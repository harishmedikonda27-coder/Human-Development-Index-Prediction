# Problem Statement

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a widely used indicator developed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) to measure a country's overall development. It evaluates three key dimensions:

* Health (Life Expectancy)
* Education (Expected Years of Schooling and Mean Years of Schooling)
* Standard of Living (Gross National Income per Capita)

Traditional methods of assessing development often rely on manual analysis of large datasets, making the process time-consuming and difficult for quick decision-making.

The objective of this project is to develop a Machine Learning model capable of predicting the Human Development Index (HDI) score using socio-economic indicators. The application provides an easy-to-use web interface where users can enter the required parameters and instantly receive the predicted HDI score along with its corresponding Human Development category.

The project aims to support students, researchers, policymakers, and analysts in understanding the relationship between development indicators and overall human development.
