Human Development Index (HDI)

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical measure developed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) to evaluate the overall development of countries. It combines three major dimensions of human development:

  • Life Expectancy at Birth
  • Expected Years of Schooling
  • Mean Years of Schooling
  • Gross National Income (GNI) Per Capita

This web application uses a Machine Learning model built with Linear Regression to estimate the Human Development Index based on the values entered by the user.

Click the Predict button to enter the country details and generate the predicted HDI score along with its development category.
