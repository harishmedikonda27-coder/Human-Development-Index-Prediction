# User Manual

## Home Page

Launch the application to view the project introduction and overview of the Human Development Index (HDI).

Click the **Predict** button to navigate to the prediction page.

---

## Prediction Page

Enter the required details:

* Country
* Life Expectancy
* Expected Years of Schooling
* Mean Years of Schooling
* Gross National Income (GNI) per Capita

After entering the values, click the **Predict HDI** button.

---

## Result Page

The application displays:

* Predicted HDI Score
* Human Development Category

The category may be one of the following:

* Very High Human Development
* High Human Development
* Medium Human Development
* Low Human Development

Click **Predict Again** to return to the prediction page and perform another prediction.

---

## Deployment

The application is deployed on Render and can be accessed online through the live demo link without requiring local installation.
